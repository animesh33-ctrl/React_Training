Margin Utilities

Tailwind provides margin utility classes to control spacing outside elements.

1. m

m: Adds margin on all four sides of an element.

Example: m-4 → Adds equal margin on top, right, bottom, and left.

2. mx

mx: Adds horizontal margin (left and right).

Example: mx-4 → Margin-left and margin-right applied.

3. my

my: Adds vertical margin (top and bottom).

Example: my-4 → Margin-top and margin-bottom applied.

4. mt

mt: Adds margin only to the top.

Example: mt-4 → Top margin added.

5. mr

mr: Adds margin only to the right.

Example: mr-4 → Right margin added.

6. mb

mb: Adds margin only to the bottom.

Example: mb-4 → Bottom margin added.

7. ml

ml: Adds margin only to the left.

Example: ml-4 → Left margin added.

8. mx-auto

mx-auto: Sets left and right margins to auto.

Commonly used to center block elements horizontally.

Example: mx-auto → Centers a fixed-width element.
